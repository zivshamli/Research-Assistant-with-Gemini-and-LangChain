Onions are bulb-shaped vegetables that belong to the Allium family, which also includes garlic, shallots, leeks, and chives. They are widely cultivated and used as a staple ingredient in cuisines around the world. Onions are known for their distinct flavor, which can range from mild and sweet to pungent and spicy, depending on the variety and how they are prepared.
